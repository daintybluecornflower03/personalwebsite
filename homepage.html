ABOUT ME

I'm a Information Management Student

I am currently pursuing my studies at Universiti Teknologi MARA (UiTM) Segamat in the Information Management programme. I am interested in information organisation, records management, and managing information systems. I am a responsible and organised individual who is willing to learn and develop practical skills for my future career.



My name is Ieshatul Radhiah binti Muttmaein. I was born at Segamat Hospital and raised in Segamat, Johor. I was born on 10 March 2005. I am the eldest of three siblings, with one younger brother and one younger sister.

I am an introverted individual with an INFJ personality type. It takes a little time for me to become comfortable and build close connections with new people, but I value meaningful relationships and clear communication.

During my free time, I usually spend time watching anime, Chinese historical dramas, or movies, as they help me relax and unwind. I also enjoy reading books, especially action, mystery, and science fiction genres, as I like stories that are engaging, thought-provoking, and allow me to imagine different worlds and possibilities. Reading and watching shows have become my way to recharge and enjoy some quiet time on my own.
